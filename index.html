    Why Susclo?

    Planet Earth is dying and we are killing it. Our behaviours are causing our planet to become a place in which, if we do not change, we will not be able to live. It is said that oil is our most valuable dwindling resource but it is water that will quickly become the resource that is most needed

    We can live without oil but we cant live without water. Our behaviours have to change if we want to stall this pending. Susclo Sustainable Clothing wants to be part of the solution, not contribute to the problem. We also aim to educate our customers on how their purchasing habits affect the planet and how they can change to ensure that there is a planet for future generations.
