Are we trying to destroy our planet

Oil is not our most valuable resource.Clean water is. Fast Fashion uses the water that should be available for drinking water.
Air pollution kills thousands of people every year. Fast fashion contributes to this pollution in its production processes.
Production processes in the manufacture of fast fashion contribute to global warming which in turn is leading to many species of animal disappearing from our planet.
